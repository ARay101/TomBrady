Tom Brady

This is Tom Brady, one of the best American football players!

Tom Brady is an amazing American football player, possibly the best of all time, playing with the New England Patriots from 2000 to 2019, and with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers from 2020 onwards.

Biography

Early life and college carrer

Tom Brady was born in San Mateo, California., on August 3, 1977. At a young age, his favorite football player was Joe Montana.
He went to college at the University of Michigan, where he played as backup quarterback. In 1997, his team went undefeated and went on to win the Rose Bowl. He became the starting quarterback from the 1998 to 1999 seasons.

New England Patriots

Tom Brady was chosen by the New England Patriots in 2000. He was a back-up quarterback. In 2001, his team won Super Bowl 36, and was named MVP. In 2002, Bredsoe left the team, which made Tom Brady starting quarterback, but this season the Patriots did not make it to the playoffs.

In 2003 and 2004, the team made it to Super Bowl 38 and 39, winning and getting MVP in both. The Patriots didn't make it to the Super Bowl in 2005 and 2006, and got a near perfect season in 2007, before barely losing Super Bowl 42. In 2008, leg injuries forced Brady to miss out most of the season.

In 2009, Brady came back form his injuries, and tied the record for biggest margin since 1940 in a game. However, they lost the wild card game. In 2011, they went to Super Bowl 46, but eventually lost the game.

In 2014, the Patriots won Super Bowl 49 in a 4th quarter comeback, ending their 11 year Super Bowl win drought. He got MVP. In 2015, he participated in a scandal, Deflategate, which made him miss the first 4 games of the 2016 season. Once his timeout was over, he helped the team win Super Bowl 51 in overtime.

In 2017 and 2018, he lost and won Super Bowls 51 and 52. In 2019, his last year as a Patriot, he lost the wild card game. He did not resign with the Patriots, and eventually signed with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

Tampa Bay Buccanneers

In 2020, he led the team to an 11-5 season, then winning post-game matches to see who will get in the Super Bowl, upsetting each team. In Super Bowl 55, the Buccaneers won against the Chiefs 31-9. He got MVP. He plans to play with the Bucs for future seasons.
